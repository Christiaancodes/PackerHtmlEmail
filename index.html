PACKER FOR REEBOK CLUB C 85

When it comes to the Reebok Club C, there’s nothing quite like a pair in white leather. From the original 1985 version up and across all the iterations that have released since, it has always been the go-to look when it comes to the ever-versatile silhouette.

For Packer’s contribution to that illustrious legacy, the classic white leather Club C comes together like never before, using mismatched upper panels that alternate between smooth premium garment leather and full grain tumbled leather.

A burgundy lining and accents combine with shades of warm grey and rich cream to contrast the intertwining crisp white textures. Other Packer touches include tonal-ed out tongue labels and side panel windows as well as co-branded insoles.
